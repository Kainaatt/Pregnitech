 [Medical Monitoring]

- Schedule routine check-ups every 4 weeks.
- Discuss any persistent pain or unusual symptoms.
- Monitor blood pressure and weight gain.
- Doppler ultrasound may detect heartbeat.
- Ultrasound may reveal fetal anatomy, gender.
- Ask about necessary supplements beyond prenatal vitamins.
- Discuss vaccinations if needed.
- Check for infections impacting fetus.
- Seek care if vaginal bleeding, severe cramping, or unusual discharge occurs.
- Blood tests may assess iron, glucose, thyroid.
- Rh factor discussion if mother is Rh-negative.
- Continue monitoring for gestational diabetes risk.
- Track fetal growth milestones via scans.
- Ask doctor about safe medications.
- Emergency protocols: fainting, dizziness, abdominal pain.
